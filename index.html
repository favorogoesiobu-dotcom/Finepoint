Our Story

Fifty Years of Fine Craft

Since 1976, Fine Point has been dedicated to a single idea: that the tools we write with should be as considered as the words themselves.

What began as a small workshop for fountain pen restoration has grown into a house of luxury stationery, trusted by writers, treasured by artists, and cherished by the next generation just learning to hold a pencil.

Every piece we make is built to be kept — refilled, reused, and passed down, rather than replaced.
